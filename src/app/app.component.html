About Me

Hi! My name is Kaylee Maclure and I'm a Graphic Designer and Illustrator originally from the sleepy leaf peeping state of Vermont with a taste for nerdy & creative hobbies. You want to talk horror movies or have a discussion about the latest video game? I'm your person. Being a creative at heart, I'm always seaking the next crafting project whether it be painting or crochet. Overall, an open minded person with an interest in many things except for sports, unless we are talking esports.

As for experience, I have a Bachelors in Graphic Design along with 6 years of experience in the industry. I started off working as a Freelancer, then a Graphic Designer and Print Production Specialist at CW Creative, to currently working as a jack-of-all-trades Graphic Designer with a not-for-profit insurance advirsory association called AAIS. My future goals include gaining a firm grasp of Java Script, furthering my experience with video editing, and working my way to the position of Creative Director in the future. Currently I have familiarity with video production, animation, HTML, CSS, and a firm understanding of UI/UX and web design. My favorite project is logo or branding design where I'm able to thoroughly utilize my talent for illustration. When I don't have my graphic design hat on, I'm a crafty creative at heart who has a pentient for horror and comedy movies.
